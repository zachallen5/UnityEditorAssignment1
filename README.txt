-The game will load into a 2D orthographic camera looking overhead the scene. 
-Use the arrow keys to move the main character
-Press ‘M’ to bring up the prefabs menu. To spawn an item onto the map, simply click the prefab you wish to spawn then click anywhere on the map.
-To get an up close look of your map, press ‘C’ to switch to a player-parented 3D perspective camera! 
-After you have built your level and wish to save, press ‘S’.
-If you want to restart your level, press ‘P’ to destroy all objects. 
-If you want to load a previously saved level, press ‘L’.
